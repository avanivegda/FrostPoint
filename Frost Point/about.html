HISTORY


The origins of the Frost Point journey dates back to 1985. Shri Mohan patel working as a ground enginer when his love for food blossomed. By night, he started creating his very own ice-cream using a combination of pure milk, unique ingredients and his passion for flavor. His legacy lives on today and over thirty years along the journey our endeavour remains the same.



Times Change, Values Endure

The late 90s saw the second generation-Pradeep patel joining the business started by his father. this time, Frost Point had also established a name for itself in this business. An innovator with natural business instincts, Pradeep has been instrumental in making Frost Point one of the fastest growing ice cream brands. For the last two decades, he has been leading the company through changing times, tastes and trends.
